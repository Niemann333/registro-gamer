The Options/City tune has the drums cut out for
a while on the first loop. Likely a bug, but it
occurs on real hardware.

This game uses a particularly nasty version of
Nu Romantic's pervasive music system that
ended up on just about every Western-developed
game after 1992. This seems to be an early
version, as it's REALLY easy to kick it out of
sync, even just during normal gameplay... -DJSW
