Notes:
This is the first set tested under in_vgm 0.32.
Now it reports song lengths in mm:ss format,
making my job of writing these readmes much,
much easier.

This soundtrack utterly kicks ass. I personally
hold Jesper Kyd up there as a Squarewave God
along with Tim and Geoff Follin. Unlike those
two, however, his tunes are exceptionally
lengthy - and he's still around. If you've
played a Hitman game, the music was done by
Jesper Kyd. The upcoming Unreal Engine 3 games,
UT2007 (UT being my favorite FPS to obsessively
play) and Gears of War will also be done by him.
Awesome stuff through and through. Be sure to
give each song a full playthrough!

The set sounds like ass with Overdrive on - it
clips like hell. Sorry kids! You'll have to 
turn up your speakers. :(

Odd enough, Two-Face's Theme is exactly 3:33.3
seconds. Just a little fun fact worth nothing.
