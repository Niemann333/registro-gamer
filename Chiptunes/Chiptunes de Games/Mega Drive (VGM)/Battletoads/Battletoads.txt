Notes:
This game is awesome! Challenge at its goddamn
best.

On real hardware, I've only beat this game once.
I never owned it, but it took me and my brother
quite a few rents before we could actually play
this right. It's pretty much impossible to beat
it with two players though. Ugh. Hard.

Anyway, great music here, same as the NES
version, but better. The NES one does has its
own charms, though... My favourite here is
"Ragnarok's Canyon." This is definitely a song
some band should cover, but without ruining the
feeling and everything, like bands usually do.

By the way, I had to hack the game with Kega to
rip this right. I've made a simple pause hack
that freezed the gameplay when the level
started. This is how I've got songs to play
without any SFX, and that means the sound on
the beginning of some of these tracks are truly
part of the song.

And to be perfectly honest, I've also hacked
lives and the life bar. No way I'd play this
fair again. Hell no. +__+

Anyway, enjoy!

- Duke
